# Schoterpoort Medical Centre -- Information in English

## Staffing Notice (May 2025)

## General Practitioner Services

The GP (General Practitioner) is a specialist in general medicine and primary care, serving as the primary healthcare contact in the Netherlands. The role encompasses examining acute and chronic conditions, performing minor surgical procedures, providing hospital referrals, and gatekeeping hospital access. GPs have direct access to diagnostic services including imaging, laboratory testing, and cardiac/pulmonary function testing.

## Multidisciplinary Team

Schoterpoort operates as an integrated multidisciplinary team including healthcare assistants, practice nurses, and mental health workers. The practice coordinates closely with hospitals, physical therapists, midwives, and social services to provide comprehensive care.

## Services Offered

### Practice Nurses (Praktijkverpleegkundige / PVK)

Practice nurses manage chronic disease monitoring programmes including:

- **Diabetes** monitoring and management
- **COPD** monitoring and management
- **Hypertension** (high blood pressure) monitoring and management

They also perform wound care, injections, cervical screening (PAP tests), ECGs, and pulmonary function testing.

### Mental Health Workers

Address mild mental health conditions through counselling and e-health options.

### Repeat Prescriptions

Available 24/7 via phone (menu option 2) or online via the patient portal. Prescriptions are dispensed within two working days.

### Additional Services

- Email consultations and online appointment booking
- Home visits when medically necessary
- Telephone consultations with callback service
