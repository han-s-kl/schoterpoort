## Praktijk Steketee is currently accepting new patients

Praktijk Steketee is currently accepting new patients. Do you live in postal code area 2021 through 2026 and do not yet have a GP in the region? Then you can register online [here](https://huisartsenpraktijk-steketee.mijnpraktijk.nl/register).

## General information

The practice has a maximum patient capacity. It may happen that the practice temporarily cannot accept new patients to prevent organisational overload. Exceptions are made for family expansion and cohabitation with already registered patients. The practice aims to keep at least one location open for new registrations from postcode areas 2021, 2022, 2023 and 2024.

## How do you register?

Contact the practice assistants to register. If you are accepted, you will receive a registration form with practice information. After the medical records from your previous GP have been received, an introductory consultation with the GP will be scheduled.
