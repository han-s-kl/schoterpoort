# Complaints procedure

Most patients are satisfied with the care provided. If you do have a complaint or concern, you are encouraged to discuss it with your GP first and work towards a solution together.

The practice takes complaints seriously and uses them for quality improvement. When a complaint form is submitted via the website, the internal complaints coordinator ensures it is processed. Patients receive an acknowledgement of receipt and are contacted about the next steps, which may include discussions with the staff members involved.

## Submitting a complaint

Do you have a complaint? You can submit it by sending an email to [info@schoterpoort.com](mailto:info@schoterpoort.com) with "Complaint" in the subject line. Please include:

- Your first and last name
- Address, postcode and city
- Telephone number
- Email address
- Your complaint
