# Complaints procedure

Most patients are satisfied with the care provided. If you do have a complaint or concern, you are encouraged to discuss it with your GP first and work towards a solution together.

This also applies to complaints about a practice assistant, practice nurse, mental health practitioner, or practice organisation. Once the GP is aware of your concerns, a solution can often be found together.

The practice takes complaints seriously - first and foremost for you personally, but also because they can contribute to improving the quality of our practice.

## Submitting a complaint

When you submit a complaint via ZIVVER the complaints coordinator will ensure it is processed within the practice. You will receive confirmation of receipt and be contacted about the most appropriate next steps - for example a conversation with the GP or staff member concerned.

## External complaints handling

If you do not wish to proceed internally, or we cannot resolve the issue together, you can contact an independent complaints officer at DOkH (complaints and disputes department).
