# Administration form

This form is intended to report changed address and insurance details to the practice assistant. It cannot be used to register yourself as a new patient with one of the GPs without prior consultation.

[Download the administration form (PDF)](/schoterpoort/documents/Administratieformulier.pdf)

## Common requests

- Transfer of medical records (see [Medical records](/schoterpoort/en/medisch-dossier))
- Viewing your records
- Changing your details
- Requesting a certificate or statement (see [Rates](/schoterpoort/en/tarieven-2025))
