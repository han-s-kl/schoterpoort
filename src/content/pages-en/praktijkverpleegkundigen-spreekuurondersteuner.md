The consultation support worker is a specially trained medical assistant. At our practice, Marjoleine fulfils this role.

She can independently manage a number of common non-serious conditions such as insect bites, bite wounds, scrapes and cuts, tooth through lip, toe injuries, nosebleeds, sore throats, cold and sinus complaints, and sprained ankles.
