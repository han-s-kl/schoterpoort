# Patient portal

Via the patient portal, you can securely and easily manage matters online with your general practice. You can, among other things:

- Make and cancel appointments
- Request repeat prescriptions
- Send e-consultations
- View test results
- View your medical records

## Log in to your portal

Choose your practice to log in:

- [Practice Louwet / Moes](https://praktijklouwet.uwzorgonline.nl)
- [Practice Duijn / Schouten](https://praktijkduijn.uwzorgonline.nl)
- [Practice Van der Eem / Bosch](https://praktijkvandereem.uwzorgonline.nl)
- [Practice Steketee / Schaap](https://praktijksteketee.uwzorgonline.nl)

## Important

- The patient portal is available for patients **aged 16 and over**
- Unfortunately, it is not possible to create an account for your child due to privacy legislation
- **Is your child under 16?** Use the [contact form for children](/schoterpoort/en/contact-kinderen) to send a secure message
- **Important:** Do not log in to your own patient portal to ask a question about your child. Your portal is linked to your own records, not those of your child

## Need help?

Are you experiencing problems creating or logging into your account? Please contact the **Digital Healthcare Helpdesk**:

- **Telephone:** [085-1304575](tel:085-1304575)
- **Website:** [helpdeskdigitalezorg.nl](https://helpdeskdigitalezorg.nl/zorginstelling/huisartsen-zuid-kennemerland/)

The helpdesk also assists you with other digital healthcare services, such as DigiD, online appointments and health apps.

**Please make sure you select the correct practice/GP. Otherwise, we cannot process your registration.**
