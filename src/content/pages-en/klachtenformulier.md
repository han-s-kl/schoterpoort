# Complaints form

Do you have a complaint about our care or service? We would like to hear about it. A complaint helps us to improve our care.

## How do you submit a complaint?

1. **Discuss it first** -- Often a conversation with your GP or the practice manager is the quickest route to a solution.
2. **Submit in writing** -- Send your complaint by email to [info@schoterpoort.com](mailto:info@schoterpoort.com) with "Complaint" in the subject line.

## External complaints handling

Cannot reach a resolution together? Then you can contact the independent complaints committee:

- **DOkH** (De Onafhankelijke klachtencommissie voor Huisartsen)
- Website: [www.dokh.nl](https://www.dokh.nl)

More information can be found on our [complaints procedure](/schoterpoort/en/klachtenregeling) page.
