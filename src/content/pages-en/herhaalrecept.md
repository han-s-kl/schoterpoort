# Requesting a repeat prescription

Repeat prescriptions for chronic medication can be requested 24 hours a day (including weekends) in 2 ways:

## 1. Via the prescription line (telephone)

Call the practice phone number of your own GP and select **option 2**.

- Practice M. Louwet: [023 -- 525 43 02](tel:0235254302)
- Practice M.M. Duijn: [023 -- 525 54 03](tel:0235255403)
- Practice W.H. Steketee: [023 -- 525 62 31](tel:0235256231)
- Practice L. van der Eem: [023 -- 525 33 04](tel:0235253304)

The prescription line will automatically ask you the following questions:

- The name of your GP
- Name and date of birth of the person the prescription is intended for
- Street and house number of the home address
- Name(s) of the medication you are ordering (have the boxes to hand!)
- Name of your pharmacy

## 2. Via the patient portal

You can also request repeat prescriptions online via the patient portal on the website. You will need to register first. Please note that after creating an account, it may take up to 7 days before the account is active.

## Which medication can you request?

You can only request prescriptions for medication that you use **chronically** (usually daily) on the advice of your GP or specialist.

If you wish to request medication for newly occurring complaints or temporary medication -- which you received for a specific period -- you **cannot** order these through the prescription line or website. For this, you can call the practice assistant during office hours; if necessary, your GP will be consulted about the request.

## When will your medication be ready?

If you leave a message on the prescription line or order a prescription via the website **before 12:00 noon**, you can collect your medication from your pharmacy **2 working days later** (they will be ready from 15:00).

Examples:
- If you call/email on Tuesday evening, your medication will be ready at the pharmacy on **Friday**.
- If you call/email on Friday afternoon after 12:00 or at the weekend, you can collect your medication on **Wednesday after 15:00**.
