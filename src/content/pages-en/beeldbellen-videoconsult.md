# Video calling: video consultation with the GP

The practice offers video consultations with GPs and practice support staff via a secure connection. These appointments last approximately 10 minutes, comparable to a consultation at the practice.

## How does it work?

You can request a video consultation through the practice assistant. You will receive a link via email or text message to access the secure video platform. 10 minutes before your appointment, click the link and log in to test your audio and video settings before the appointment begins.

## What do you need?

- A computer, tablet or smartphone with a camera and sound
- An internet connection
- The ability to share images if needed

## Benefits

Video consultations save travel time, eliminate the need for childcare and avoid waiting room time. You can consult with your healthcare provider from home or work.

## Limitations

Not all health complaints are suitable for a video consultation - physical examination cannot be performed remotely.

## Privacy

Your conversation is not recorded. The consultations use the practice's secure internet connection. Healthcare providers work in closed rooms to prevent eavesdropping and to ensure standard confidentiality. We also advise you to use a private space and to avoid public Wi-Fi networks.

## Technical support

If you experience login problems, please contact the practice assistant. If the video connection drops, the doctor will try to reach you by phone.
