# Cookie policy

## What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file sent to your browser, allowing you to be automatically recognised on repeat visits to the website. Cookies can collect personal data linked to website usage, including pages visited and IP addresses.

## Functional cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function. The practice uses:

| Cookie | Purpose | Retention period |
|--------|---------|-----------------|
| fontResizeCookie | Stores the chosen font size | 1 month |
| demopraktijk_secure | Enables login and stores personal settings | Duration of the visit |
| smbv_splash_practice | Indicates that cookies have been accepted | 1 year |

## Social media

The website may contain sharing buttons for platforms such as Facebook and Twitter. Although the practice itself does not place cookies through these buttons, the social networks place their own cookies. The practice has no influence over this. Please refer to the privacy policy of each platform for more information.

## YouTube

Embedded YouTube videos place cookies. These originate from YouTube, not from the practice. The practice cannot influence their placement, use or blocking.

## Managing cookies

You can adjust your browser settings to block cookies entirely. However, disabling all cookies may cause certain website features to stop working properly.
