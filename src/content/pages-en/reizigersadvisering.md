# Travel health advice

When planning a long-distance trip, additional precautions may be necessary, such as vaccinations.

You can contact the following organisations for travel advice and travel vaccinations:

## GGD Kennemerland

- **Address:** Zijlweg 200, 2015 CK Haarlem
- **Telephone:** 023 -- 789 1616
- **Website:** [www.ggdreisvaccinaties.nl](https://www.ggdreisvaccinaties.nl)
- [GGD Kennemerland location Haarlem](https://www.ggdreisvaccinaties.nl/locaties/ggd-kennemerland-locatie-haarlem)

## Meditel

- **Locations:** Amsterdam and Alkmaar
- **Telephone:** 0900 -- 2021040
- **Website:** [meditel.nl](https://meditel.nl/portaal/index.php/opreis/vestigingen/2015KT)

## More information

Information about travel advice is also available from the [National Coordination Centre for Travellers' Health Advice (LCR)](http://www.lcr.nl).
