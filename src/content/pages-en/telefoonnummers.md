## Emergencies

**Weekdays (Mon-Fri 08:00-17:00):** Call the number of your own GP. Press 1 for life-threatening situations. In case of immediate danger: [112](tel:112)

**Evenings, nights, weekends, public holidays:** Call the GP Emergency Post: [023-750 4567](tel:023-7504567)

The Emergency Post provides care for health problems that need to be assessed within a few hours. Two locations:
- Vondelweg 999 (Haarlem Noord)
- Boerhaavelaan 22 (Haarlem Zuid) at Spaarne Gasthuis

## Repeat prescriptions
