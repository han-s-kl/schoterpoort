Huisartsenpraktijk Schoterpoort is an accredited training practice of the University GP Institute and the basic medical training programme of Amsterdam UMC. The GP trainees have been qualified doctors for some time and usually have several years of experience working in hospitals and general practices.

GP trainees are trained in the practices of M. Louwet and W.H. Steketee; they are in the first year of their training. As of 1 March 2026, these are Jesse Holverda and Lazin Germawi. In the practices of doctor Louwet and doctor Steketee, junior medical students also complete a short two-week placement once a year.
