# Home visit

Home visits are intended for seriously ill patients or for patients who have difficulty walking.

Examination at the practice offers better diagnostic options than a home visit. Patients are asked to request home visits before 11:00 in the morning when possible, so that the schedule can be adjusted accordingly.
