## Quality

The practice has held the NPA quality mark since 2006, renewed in early 2025 for a further three years. Patient satisfaction scores are consistently 8.5 to 9 out of 10 for physicians, staff, service and organisation.

The four GPs (Louwet, Duijn, Steketee, Van der Eem) form a partnership with 4 employed GPs and 2 GP trainees, totalling 10 physicians. The practice is an accredited training practice for AMC (UMC Amsterdam) GP trainees and medical students.

The practice emphasises service orientation, collaboration and providing high-quality patient care. Patients are registered with one of the four practice owners and work with a fixed team of GP, assistant and practice nurse. The practice coordinates care with specialists, hospitals, pharmacists, physiotherapists, psychologists and district nurses. Evening, weekend and night shifts are handled via the GP Emergency Post.

## Did you know?

- You do not need to report to the front desk for scheduled appointments; wait in the waiting room of your GP or practitioner
- Patient portal available for ordering prescriptions yourself, e-consultations and making appointments
- Appointments only by telephone or via the patient portal to protect privacy
- Assistants are trained in triage to provide appropriate care at the right time
- A national shortage of medical assistants may cause longer telephone waiting times; the team appreciates your patience
- A standard consultation is 15 minutes for one complaint; request a double consultation for multiple complaints or mental health issues
- If you suspect a bladder infection: leave a urine sample in the cooler at the entrance with a completed form
- Reliable health information at thuisarts.nl; use moetiknaardedokter.nl to assess whether a GP visit is necessary
