# Consultations

Consultation appointments require calling in advance by telephone. The practice can be reached by telephone between 8:00 and 17:00. Between 12:00-14:00 and 16:30-17:00, only urgent matters are handled by telephone.

Video consultations with doctors are available when appropriate. Mental health practice support workers can also conduct online sessions. All video consultations use secure connections.

Same-day emergency appointments are guaranteed for urgent cases. Non-urgent consultations should be scheduled 1-2 days in advance.

The practice assistants will briefly ask about the reason for your visit in order to assess the urgency and consultation duration. They maintain professional confidentiality.

Evening consultations (17:00-19:00) are available one day per week; the day varies. The assistant can confirm which day this is.

## Callback appointments

Patients can request callback appointments to avoid waiting time. Voicemail messages should be ended with '#'. The telephone system is available 24 hours for repeat prescriptions and appointment cancellations.

## Cancellation

Please cancel your appointment at least 24 hours in advance (48 hours for mental health support workers) so that others can use your slot.
