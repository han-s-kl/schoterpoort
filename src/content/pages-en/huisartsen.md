# GPs

## M. (Margot) Louwet
**GP, BIG registration number 49911135001**

Studied medicine and worked in pulmonology before completing GP training. Completed her final training year with Peter de Groof at Schoterpoort. Gained experience as a locum in regional practices. Has been a permanent locum at Schoterpoort since 2014. Took over the practice of De Groof in 2022. Works on Monday, Tuesday, Thursday morning and Friday.

"As a GP, I find building trust with patients and finding suitable health solutions very important." Enjoys following patients over the long term through good and difficult times. Leisure: outdoor activities with family, gym, inline skating, swimming, concerts, theatre, cooking.

## N.M. (Natasja) Moes
**GP, BIG registration number 09045639001**

Interested in psychiatric aspects of medicine.

Lives in Overveen with husband and three children (daughter studying medicine in Leiden, two sons). Hobbies: sculpting, graphic art, singing in an English cathedral choir, flute ensemble, piano lessons, gardening, walking in cities and nature, occasional tennis.
